How toothpaste works

In this six minute lecture, you will learn about the basic components of toothpaste. This is an in-person co-operative exercise, you will be pairing up with one learner adjacent to you. The video will inform you about when you need to teach your partner about the components you just learned. You will need each other's help in order to successfully complete the video. For the best viewing experience, we recommend going fullscreen.
